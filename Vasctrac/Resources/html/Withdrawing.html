You can withdraw from the study at any time and we will stop collecting new data immediately. To withdraw, click “Withdraw from Study” on the My Profile screen.

Any study data collected before you withdraw will still be used in the study and will not be deleted. This allows us to reconnect your data to your account if you change your mind and rejoin. If you wish to re-enroll, simply install the app, presst “Previously Enrolled” and log in using your account info.

The study team may also withdraw you from the study at any time or for any reason.
